Break-even math: Total monthly nut (expenses + debt) = $3,462 + $1,667 = $5,129/mo. Each room at $200/wk = $867/mo. $5,129 ÷ $867 = 5.92 rooms. So 6 of 9 rooms occupied covers every expense + debt service. That leaves 3 rooms of pure profit margin. At 91% occupancy (8.19 rooms paying), the cushion is 2.27 rooms above break-even.
