Title Transfer (Not Deed) — DHSMV Process
FL mobile homes are titled via DHSMV like vehicles, not via deed. Title transfers at final RTO payment. Jay must use FL-compliant Lease-Option or Installment Sale structure. Lease-option = eviction on default (15–30 days); installment sale may require foreclosure. Get FL attorney review before marketing any unit.
Steve Levy Partnership Dependency
Jay’s income flows from Steve’s homes. If Steve changes terms, exits the partnership, or restricts Jay’s marketing rights, Jay’s income stops. Partnership terms should be documented in writing — specifically: Jay’s right to market, collect RTO payments, split structure, and what happens at note payoff.
55+ HOPA 80/20 Rule
At least 80% of occupied units must have at least one resident 55+. Confirm with Steve exactly how many non-55 slots are available before marketing to younger buyers. Labor-trade candidates (roofers, handymen) may be under 55 — verify slot availability per unit first.
Park Approval for RTO Sales Required
Confirm in writing that Park Avenue MHP allows RTO / owner-finance sales and has a resident approval process Jay can navigate. Get the buyer approval criteria, transfer fees (if any), and timeline before the first open house. No marketing to buyers who can’t pass park approval.
