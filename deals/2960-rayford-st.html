Title Warning + Deal Overview

Read This Before Anything Else
!Recorded Title Contradicts PropStream's Ownership Claim

PropStream names the owner as "Cuttyhunk LLC" and shows a $257,017 sale dated 5/2/2024. Duval County's actual recorded title shows no such deed. The most recent recorded instrument is a $275,000 Warranty Deed dated 11/27/2023 to Michelle De La Pena — the same owner as the other two properties in this pipeline, and the same closing date. Cuttyhunk LLC does not appear anywhere in the chain of title.

Cuttyhunk LLC itself is heavily anonymized — an active Florida LLC (filed 4/18/2024) registered at a commercial mail-drop, managed by two more anonymous Wyoming LLCs ("Hestia Holdings LLC" and "Yeet Industries LLC"). No natural person is named anywhere in its public filing, and nothing in that filing connects it to Shell or to De La Pena.

Before drafting or signing anything: order a title search / O&E (Title America or Precision Title), and ask Shell directly for documentation proving her authority to sell — whether that's on De La Pena's behalf or Cuttyhunk LLC's. The financial analysis below assumes De La Pena is the real seller, since that's what the county confirms — treat it as provisional.
