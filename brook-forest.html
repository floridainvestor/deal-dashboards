Risk Flags

Know before you sign
Thin equity spread — $10K cushion
Net equity at close is only $10,064. One major repair (roof, HVAC, plumbing) could wipe out 50–100% of first-year gains. Inspect aggressively during the 3-day window.
LTR alone doesn't pencil at $45K
If MTR/STR fails or zoning doesn't allow it, you're stuck at 6–9% TWR — a weak return for $46K cash out. The entire deal thesis depends on alternative rental execution.
32208 C-class neighborhood risk
NW Jacksonville — higher vacancy rates, maintenance profile, and tenant quality risk on LTR. MTR/STR demand needs confirmation — not all C-class zips support $3,500/mo mid-term.
May vacancy gap
HUD tenant leaves May 2026. If MTR/STR setup isn't ready to go live that month, you carry $1,348/mo PITI with zero revenue. Need furniture, listing, and platform ready before close.
DOSC — Due-On-Sale Clause
5.87% rate is decent but not exceptional. Lender's incentive to call the loan is moderate. Mitigate with land trust vesting. Monitor servicer communications closely post-close.
Seller's title company
"Must close with seller's title company" — not all title companies understand sub-to/land trust structure. Confirm they can execute correctly. If not, this is a deal-breaker to negotiate.
Taxes & insurance unconfirmed
PITI is estimated. If actual taxes are $300/mo instead of $250/mo, LTR cash flow goes negative even self-managed. Pull Duval PAO and get a real insurance quote before committing.
HUD income bridge — confirmed through May
$1,500/mo guaranteed until end of May lease. Cash flows from day 1 of ownership. Gives 4–6 weeks to set up MTR/STR without a revenue gap if you close in April.

3 Decisions Jay Needs to Make

1
Is $3,500/mo MTR or $4,000+/mo STR realistic in 32208?
This is the only question that matters. If yes — the deal works at $45K entry and hits near 30% CoC. If uncertain — do not sign until confirmed. Run PadSplit comps and Airbnb occupancy data for the ZIP before the EMD goes hard.
2
Push for $35K entry before signing — or accept $45K?
At $35K entry: MTR CoC jumps from 29% to 37%. Seller is losing $1,500/mo in May. Use that urgency. Counter at $32K, land at $35K. Every $5K off is worth 7–8 CoC points and reduces exposure if the revenue strategy underperforms.
3
Can you have MTR/STR live before or immediately after the May vacancy?
The HUD tenant leaves in May. If you close in April, you get 4–6 weeks of HUD income to set up the alternative rental. Don't let there be a revenue gap. Furnishings, listing photos, platform setup, and pricing must be ready to flip on the day the tenant walks out.
