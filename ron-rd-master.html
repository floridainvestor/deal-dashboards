Risk Flags
Quadplex Claim is FALSE
Seller claims 10-12 BD quadplex expansion. Lot is 6,948 sqft; RMD-B requires 14,800 sqft for 4 units. Cannot legally build a quadplex on this lot. Seller is either uninformed or misleading. Use as negotiation leverage.
High Crime Area
32210 violent crime is 2.35x the national average. Lackawanna pocket has 88.3% childhood poverty rate. Property damage risk and tenant safety concerns for PadSplit model.
Tight Room-to-SF Ratio
1,382 sqft for 7 rooms = 197 sqft/room average. Very tight for PadSplit livability standards. 5 rooms may be more realistic. Walk required before committing.
Seller $74K Underwater
DSG GROUP LLC paid $229,900 in March 2025. At $156K counter, seller loses $74K. Leverage: they're already listing at a $40K loss — they know the position. $6K cash + debt relief is better than foreclosure.
Break-Even at 71% Occupancy
Need 5 of 7 rooms filled to break even at $156K counter. Lackawanna vacancy rate is 17.7% (high). Comparable to Harts Rd which breaks even at 57% (4 of 7) — Ron Rd has less margin.
Interest Rate Unknown
PITI of $1,900/mo on $150K balance implies P&I of ~$1,393/mo. Need to verify rate, remaining term, and loan type before taking SubTo. If rate is 8%+ this is significantly less attractive. Rate is the single biggest unknown.
Fully Renovated — No Rehab
Brand new everything per seller. If true, eliminates rehab capex risk. Only conversion costs (walls, locks, furnishing). Verify quality at walk.
RMD-B Zoning Favorable
Multi-family zoning is favorable for PadSplit room rental. No HOA restrictions. Duplex conversion may also be feasible as alternative exit. Zoning supports the use case.

3
Request Mortgage Statement Before Signing
"Before I can commit, I need to see the most recent mortgage statement — balance, rate, term, servicer, current/past-due status. A $1,900 PITI on $150K could mean a high rate, PMI, or escrow shortage. I need to know what I'm assuming."
This is the go/no-go gate. Do not sign without this information.

"I need to walk the property and measure the garage, living room, and dining room to confirm how many rooms are actually feasible. Seven is the target but five may be the realistic ceiling."
